How to create a Gmail App Password
  1. Open Google Account → Security.
  2. Under How you sign in to Google, ensure 2-Step Verification is turned on. (App Passwords are only available when 2FA is active.)
  3. In the search bar at the top of that page type App passwords and open the result.
  4. Under App name type something like Career Transition Form, then click Create.
  5. Google displays a 16-character password (e.g. abcd efgh ijkl mnop). Copy it — it is shown only once.
  6. Paste it into the Gmail App Password field below (spaces are fine).

Note: if you do not see the App passwords option, your account may be managed by a Google Workspace admin who has disabled it.
